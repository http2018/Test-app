Books Online
The Libraries has over seven thousand rare books, journals and manuscripts online on subjects ranging from advertising, History
and Culture, Art and Design, and Science and Technology. Over 30,000 volumes on Natural History
Collections
The Libraries physical collections comprise 1.8 million books and manuscripts, along with over 400,000 pieces of ephemera,
microfilm, photo collections and a/v material, housed in over 20 locations.
Exhibitions
The Libraries has a history of producing both physical and online exhibitions that use our vast collections to delight and
educate. Here you can find digital exhibitons from our past as well as an index of all physical exhibitions that the Libraries has produced over the years..
